Mandalize Help

What is Mandalize?

Mandalize is a free browser-based animated mandala maker. It helps you create kaleidoscope art from drawings, images, sprites, vector shapes and animated GIFs.

Drawing with symmetry

Choose the number of axes, then draw with the brush or line tool. Your marks are repeated around the mandala centre. Toggle Mirror for a kaleidoscope look, or turn it off for rotational pinwheel-style patterns.

Stamping images and GIFs

Drag images or GIFs into the palette, select one, then stamp it onto the canvas. A semi-transparent preview shows where the repeated copies will appear.

Drawing vector shapes

Use the circle, star or polygon tool to add clean vector shapes with solid colours or animated gradients, plus dash, cap and join controls.

Animating sprites and shapes

Each stamped sprite or drawn shape can animate scale, rotation, orbit, opacity, and position using looping keyframes and easing curves. Position animation splits into a Radial control (moves straight in/out from the mandala centre) and a Tangential control (slides side to side around the ring) — the two stay fully independent no matter where you placed the shape.

Trail animation

Any drawn stroke can be turned into a fading trail: a comet-like segment sweeps along the line on a loop, with adjustable speed and trail length, instead of the whole line rendering statically.

Warp mode

Warp mode bends an image into an arc around the mandala origin, useful for circular platforms, rings and curved patterns.

Layers

The layers panel lists every stroke, shape and sprite, with an eye icon to toggle visibility and a hover highlight that shows exactly what's selected on the canvas.

Theater Mode

Theater Mode hides all UI and centres the canvas full-screen for distraction-free viewing or presenting — press Esc to exit.

Exporting

Export your artwork as a PNG image, an animated GIF or WebP, or an MP4/WebM video with ready-made presets for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X and Pinterest. Guides and selection handles are hidden during export.
