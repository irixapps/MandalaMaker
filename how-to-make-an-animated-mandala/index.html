How to Make an Animated Mandala Online

Mandalize lets you make animated mandala art directly in your browser. You can draw with radial symmetry, stamp images or animated GIFs, draw vector shapes, animate properties with keyframes and export the result.

1. Choose your symmetry axes

Set the number of mandala axes. Mandalize uses radial symmetry to repeat your drawing or stamped image around the mandala centre — toggle Mirror for a kaleidoscope look, or turn it off for a rotational pinwheel.

2. Draw, stamp, or add a shape

Use the brush tool for freehand drawing, import an image or GIF into the palette and stamp it onto the canvas, or use the circle/star/polygon tool to draw clean vector shapes with solid colours or gradients.

3. Animate the mandala

Select a stamped sprite or drawn shape and use the animation controls to keyframe scale, rotation, orbit, opacity, radial (in/out) or tangential (side-to-side) position. Linear easing is useful for smooth looping orbit animations. Drawn strokes can also get a fading trail animation instead of rendering statically.

4. Export your artwork

Export a still PNG, an animated GIF or WebP, or an MP4/WebM video with ready-made presets for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X and Pinterest. Mandalize can recommend a seamless loop length and frame rate based on the animations in your scene.
