GIF Kaleidoscope Maker

Mandalize can turn animated GIFs into kaleidoscope and mandala patterns. Import a GIF, stamp it with radial symmetry, animate scale or orbit, then export the result.

Use animated GIFs as mandala stamps

Drop an animated GIF or WebP into the palette, select it, then use the Stamp tool to place it around the mandala centre. The animation plays live in the canvas.

Create mirrored or pinwheel patterns

Use Mirror mode for a classic kaleidoscope look, or turn Mirror off for rotational pinwheel-style patterns.

Export animated GIF, WebP or video mandalas

Mandalize can export animated GIF, WebP, or MP4/WebM video, and recommends frame counts or duration for seamless looping based on your scene's animations. Video export includes presets sized for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X and Pinterest.
