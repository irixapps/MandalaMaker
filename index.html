Free Animated Mandala Maker

Mandalize is a free browser-based mandala maker for creating animated kaleidoscope art from drawings, images, sprites, vector shapes and animated GIFs.

Draw with radial symmetry, stamp images and GIFs, draw vector shapes with gradients, animate scale, orbit, radial and tangential position with keyframes, add fading trail animation to your drawings, warp images into circular arcs, and export as PNG, animated GIF/WebP or video — directly from the browser. No login. No install.

Mandalize FAQ

Is Mandalize free?

Yes. Mandalize is free to use in your browser with no login required.

What can I make with Mandalize?

You can create animated mandala art, kaleidoscope patterns, radial drawings, GIF mandalas and symmetrical image compositions using drawings, stamped images/GIFs, and vector shapes with gradients.

Can I use animated GIFs?

Yes. You can import animated GIFs or WebPs, stamp them into radial symmetry patterns, animate them, and export the result as a new animated GIF, WebP or video.

Can I export a video?

Yes. Mandalize can export MP4 or WebM video with ready-made presets for Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Twitter/X and Pinterest, including automatic letterboxing so your art isn't stretched.

Does Mandalize use AI?

No. Mandalize is a creative coding and browser-based drawing tool. It does not generate images with AI.

Can I export my mandala?

Yes. You can export still PNG images, animated GIF or WebP, and MP4/WebM video.

Do I need to install anything?

No. Mandalize runs directly in your web browser.
